HLF Trainers

HLF is a way we could give back to the community while doing something we are passionate about. Before we opened our first workout session, we decided that we would not only help our community get healthier, but that we would also give 10% to Upward Sports to help children get sponsored into sports. We are glad to say that we have helped sponsor over a dozen children into the sport of their choice.
